The house

A bathroom is the quietest room in the house — and the most honest. We furnish it with porcelain, marble and brass chosen the way a gallery chooses its sculpture.

For over two decades we have supplied architects, interior studios and private clients across the Emirates with ceramics and building finishes of gallery grade — from a single hand-glazed basin to the full stone package of a villa.

Craft & supply

From a single basin
to the whole villa

We work at both scales. Homeowners come to the gallery for one perfect piece; architects and contractors come for specification, quantity take-offs and site delivery across the UAE. Either way, the same rule applies — nothing leaves the showroom that we would not fit in our own homes.

  • Specification support for architects & interior studios
  • Slab cutting, edge profiling and book-matching
  • Site delivery and installation supervision, UAE-wide
  • After-care: glaze, stone and brass maintenance
